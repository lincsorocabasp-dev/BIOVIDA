Target Market
Why the Middle East

The Persian Gulf region relies heavily on desalinated and imported water for human consumption, in an extreme-heat climate that increases demand for constant, quality hydration.

🇦🇪
United Arab Emirates
One of the highest per-capita consumers of bottled water in the world, largely due to climate and reliance on desalination. Bottled water market estimated above USD 4 billion, with projected growth in coming years.
🇶🇦
Qatar
Expanding luxury hospitality and tourism market, with strong demand for premium hydration products in hotels and international events.
🌍
Persian Gulf (regional)
Region historically lacking its own quality freshwater sources, dependent on imports and desalination — creating structural room for certified natural mineral water of foreign origin.

General reference market data; updated, country-specific market research is recommended before commercial decisions.
